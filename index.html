About Me

Biography

Mody SY received his Ph.D. degree in Telecommunication Engineering in 2024 from Sorbonne University, Paris, France. He also holds a M.Eng. degree or Dipl. Ing., in Computer Engineering (Telecommunications and Networks) from Ecole Supérieure Polytechnique (ESP) of Dakar, Senegal, which he obtained in 2020.

Mody is currently a postdoctoral researcher in the Communication Systems Department at EURECOM.

His research interests lie in the area of wireless communications, with a special focus on physical layer fundamentals and designs for post-5G communication systems .
